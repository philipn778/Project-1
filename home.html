About

MY NAME IS PHILIP NORBERG


I have trained and competed in motocross since 2012. I have competed internationally and in Sweden, among other places I have ridden both the Swedish Championships and the European Championships.

I have also been trained by ricky carmichael, jeff emig, kenneth gundersen, jeff stanton, brian jörgensen and marcus norlen.

At the moment I compete in the MX1 class, a bit of the series and other fun competitions as my focus is on coaching. Have trained children and young people now for almost 3 years and think it's great fun.
